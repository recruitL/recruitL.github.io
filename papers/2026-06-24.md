<p>GW190814 was a remarkable gravitational-wave (GW) event: a merger between a 23 solar-mass black hole (BH) and a 2.6 solar-mass compact object, with an extreme mass ratio that is difficult to reproduce through standard isolated-binary or dynamical formation channels.
